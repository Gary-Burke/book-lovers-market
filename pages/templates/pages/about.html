Welcome to Book Lover's Market!

Our History

We started out with an idea, an idea that was formed through our collective need to look and display our own personal book library as individuals. We love to gaze upon our book collection in the convenience of our cosy home and deidicated book shelf. But what about when we are away from home? Well, that is where our community was born

Our Mission

We had a need to transfer this physicaly sense of satisfaction and fulfillment into the digital realm, so that we could view our books at any given moment from literally anywhere in the world. Once we have accomplished setting up this digital library, someone mentioned that it would be extremely convenient (and utterly beneficial) if we could also buy books on this platform and sell some of our less loved titles.

Our mission, you might ask, is then to allow Book Lovers to digitalize their libraries as well as buy and sell books from fellow Book Lovers on our platform. We aim to spread the happiness brought to us by books and by books we shall prosper!
